Game of Love

It’s 8:00 pm on a Friday night, and you’ve been sitting home all day. It has been a while since you have gone out for a drink, so you are looking to turn things up a little tonight. You decide you are going to go out to the bar and meet the love of your life. First thing to do:

Shower and wash up first. No sense in going out when you’re stinking up the place.

Brush your teeth, forget the rest. Everyone at the bar is going to be too drunk to notice.

There’s no time to wash up, every minute is precious time at the bar gone to waste.
